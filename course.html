Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Our courses have been structured to meet international standard and are accredited by reputable bodies.

Intermediate

This provision is for students willing into get admitted into our A'level programme to pursue any course of their choice. Successful completion of this stage qualifies candidate with the minimum requirements to be admitted into 200 level as undergraduates.

Undergraduate

This provision is for students who have completed their secondary education and are seeking direct admission for any of our courses. Only candidates who meet the minimum requirements are offered admission due to the large number of applications recieved every academic year.

Postgraduate

This provision is for graduatesand are looking to move into more advanced studies for Post-graduate diploma, Master of Science (MSc), Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) as the case may be.
